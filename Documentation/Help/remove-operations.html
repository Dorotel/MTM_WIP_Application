üöÄ Basic Remove Operations

1

Select Items to Remove

Click on rows in the data grid to select items for removal:

  • Single Selection: Click on a row to select one item
  • Multiple Selection: Hold Ctrl and click multiple rows
  • Range Selection: Click first row, hold Shift, click last row
2

Confirm Removal

Click the Remove button and confirm the operation:

  • Single Item: Simple confirmation dialog
  • Multiple Items: Batch operation summary with item count
  • Safety Check: Review items before final confirmation
3

Audit Trail Created

System automatically creates comprehensive audit records:

  • Transaction Log: Who removed what and when
  • User Accountability: Full user tracking for all removals
  • Recovery Information: Data needed for undo operations

‚ùì Troubleshooting

Cannot remove items - "Access Denied" error

Check with your supervisor about removal privileges. Some users may have read-only access to the Remove Tab.

Remove button is disabled

Ensure you have selected at least one item in the data grid. The Remove button is only enabled when items are selected.

Undo option not available

Undo is typically available for 24 hours after removal. Check the Transactions form for longer-term recovery options.

Data grid shows no items

Try using "Show All" to display the complete inventory, or check your filter settings.
